About

Strategic Consulting Services were founded in Bandon, West Cork by Chris Harrington and Darren Curtin. The company has a diverse range of goals namely to provide a consultation to clients in the areas of Blockchains, Autonomous things and Immersive Experiences. The joint CEOs, have respectively worked in crypto-banking and have become infatuated with AI and its limitless possibilities. Their research and desire for new applications of technology are what make them the greatest team of technological consultants in Cork County.
